Donate

Support our mission to accelerate impact of social entrepreneurs in Andhra Pradesh!

Thank you for visiting the donor’s page! Your support can make a big difference in the lives of our investees and toward the self-sustainability of UnLtd Hyderabad. We hope that you will consider a gift to support the generation of social change makers in Andhra Pradesh & Telangana!

We are seeking 47 lakh rupees to support 20 social entrepreneurs through 2014 – 15. The funds will be used as financial support for investees (35%), non-financial support for investees (35%) & overhead costs (30%). Cost of supporting / sponsoring one social entrepreneur for Our Programme support is 3.5 Lakh rupees.

To support our work, please write to us at shivani@unltdhyderabad.org or call us at +91-8897339541

Support

Apart from making generous donations to us, you can also support in following ways

  • Introduce us to potential donors or grant opportunities, investors, incubators, accelerators & experts
  • Support in arranging fund-raisers like giving-breakfasts / employee-giving programs
  • Sponsor in-kind support to us and our investees – currently seeking office infrastructure such as 12 Laptops, 2 Printers and workshop spaces
  • Create employee engagement programs to engage with grassroots social entrepreneurs in building technical or soft skills
  • Collaborate with us toward the ecosystem development in the region
  • Volunteer with UnLtd Hyderabad

We believe that your support and commitment can give our investees a genuine chance to create a viable business models and lasting social impact! Your generosity, partnership and belief in our work would immensely help us to build an ecosystem for social entrepreneurs in Andhra Pradesh & Telangana.

To support our work, please write to us at shivani@unltdhyderabad.org or call us at +91-8897339541
