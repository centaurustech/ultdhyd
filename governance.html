Governance

The three key objectives of the governing board is to guard our mission, open doors for us to important networks/institution help us with legal compliances, gain funds and other resources.

Ramesh Loganathan, President

Ramesh leads Progress Software’s largest product development laboratory, at Hyderabad, India. He has over 23 years of experience in distributed systems engineering and R&D leadership. In his current capacity, he was instrumental in transforming the Hyderabad facility from an Offshore Development Center to a Center of Excellence model, developing frontline products including the common cloud services platform. Prior to joining Progress, Ramesh was Vice President of product engineering and middleware technologies at Pramati. He led the engineering effort of Pramati’s renowned J2EE Server and Studio products.

Ramesh holds a Masters degree in Electrical Engineering - Control Systems from Anna University. He is a passionate technology evangelist. He is a regular speaker at tech seminars and conferences and an active member of the IT industry organizations NASSCOM and HYSEA (formerly ITsAP), where he helps with product and technology initiatives. He has co-authored the book “SOA Approach to Integration”.

Ramesh is adjunct faculty at IIIT-H, teaching courses on Middleware systems since 2002. Ramesh is on the Academic Council at IIIT-H, and on governing councils of the Incubators at IIIT-H, BITS – Hyderabad, and IIT Hyderabad. He is also active in the Hyderabad startup-ecosystem – driving early stage startup events for StartupSaturday., TiE Hyderabad, HYSEA, Academic incubators and Hyderabad4Innovation.

Rubina Majid, Vice President

Rubina Majid is CEO and Lead Trainer at Teaching….Touching a Better Future. She is also an Educational Consultant to Scholastic India and train teachers in educational resources published by Scholastic India. She is passionate about teaching teacher and has invested her time in building their capacities to touch a better future. Previously she was Secretary of NGO SAFA dedicated to the cause of economic empowerment of urban illiterate women and education of their children.

Manvir Singh Dandona, General Secretary

Manvir has over 10 years of experience in IT consulting and two years in CSR/Development sector at different levels. He has contributed towards process standardization and change management both as an internal, as well as external change facilitator. He has a sound understanding of the issues and challenges of organizations in the development sector working in the areas of Social Entrepreneurship, Affordable Private School, and Handloom Textile industry. He continues to help different NGOs, Social Entrepreneurs, Social Enterprises apart from his IT consulting work. He is fond of food, trekking and travelling, and experiencing new cultures.

Ashwini Suman, Joint Secretary

Ashwini is founder and Managing Director of Ascendia Technology Solutions, a Hyderabad based mid sized IT Services Company. He has built a robust foundation of growth for Ascendia with individuals who work as a cohesive unit with extraordinary business values, ethics and customer orientation. He has also founded Kaara Info Systems, which works as key knowledge partner with companies like Microsoft and Cognizant in the area of Market Research, Consulting and advisory.

Debananda Misra, Treasurer

Debananda comes with a significant belief in the potential of higher education and training to solve large challenges. He believes in the role of education to not only inform society but also to influence society to bring about large scales changes. His areas of interests are relevance of higher education and access and equality of education. He lives in Hyderabad with his wife and two children.

Debananda is currently working in the administrative function at the Indian School of Business. In his spare time, he conducts research in the area of higher education as part of his doctoral program at University College London. Prior to this, he has worked with Yahoo and GE. ‎

Jakkampudi Adiseshaiah Chowdary, Board Member

Mr. Jakkampudi Adiseshaiah Chowdary has straddled the Information Technology industry in the state of Andhra Pradesh for a quarter of a century now. JA, as he is popularly known, is one of the prime movers in establishing the IT industry in the state, and in a career spanning over 25 years, has played many key roles in various capacities in both the government and corporate sectors.

After finishing his MTech in Solid State Electronics from IIT Chennai and MSc in SK University, JA started his career at ISRO as a scientist where he was involved in designing telemetry test equipment and telemetry sub systems for Bhaskara, Rohini, and Aryabhatta, India’s premier satellites. He then worked at BHEL, where he was instrumental in developing mechanisms that facilitate monitoring of pollution levels online and process control instruments for BHEL power stations.

After switching to the corporate sector, as the President and Managing Director of PortalPlayer in India, JA pioneered the development of the chip and the codecs that went into the very first generation of Apple iPod, and brought product development in technologies like embedded software and Digital Signal Processing to Indian shores. The iPod story took PortalPlayer to a successful IPO and subsequent acquisition by the Visual Computing giant NVIDIA, where JA led the Indian team as the Managing Director. He is presently associated with Talent Sprint Private Limited and MITS Technologies as a Chairman.

JA is also on the boards various companies, including Amara Raja Electronics, Center for Organization Development and the Arora Group of Educational Institutions.

A Co-Founder and a Board member of Hyderabad Angels presently, JA has been involved with many professional corporate bodies in his long career, and has donned the roles of Chairman, Industry Development Forum for AP, Co-Chairman, FICCI (Telangana & AP), President, ADI Foundation (Anantapur Development Initiative) and President, Food 360 Foundation. He has been actively associated with the establishment and growth of several bodies like American Chamber of Commerce (AMCHAM), Hyderabad Chapter, The Indus Entrepreneurs (TiE) Hyderabad chapter, and Hyderabad Software Exporters Association (HYSEA later renamed to ITsAP).

Laurels and bouquets to his services have come in the way of several recognitions and awards. Some of these include the “Meritorious Invention Award” from NRDC, Govt of India, the HMA Award of excellence “for promoting the Indian IT Industry”, the HYSEA Award of excellence “for promoting the IT industry in AP” and Award of excellence from Telugu Association of Texas State in the USA.

Kaali Sudheer, Board Member

Kaali Sudheer has more than 15 years of art experience as assistant, assistant curator, individual curator, art collector, art gallery event manager, gallery owner, art marketing mentor, etc. He worked in art galleries both in US and India with some of the world best artists and conceptualized theme-based art shows and events. Soon after his MBA, he went to US and worked with various International Companies in the field of Investment and Financial Advisory domains. With strong passion in the arts, crafts and art forms, he worked with Art Galleries.

After coming back to India, he founded Muse Art Gallery at Marriot Hotel, Hyderabad and curated more 100 art shows promoting the art of various local, regional, national and international artists. He is also an avid trainer. He taught Art Marketing at Indian School of Business, Hyderabad by preparing interactive training modules, presentations, case studies and assignments. He also mentored Artists and students to coming up with creative themes / concepts to work of paintings.
