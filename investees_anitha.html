  • Anita Narayanasamy & Sarah Mathews
  • Sankalp Women’s Support Alliance: To support women who have suffered sexual violence with comprehensive counselling, medical and legal support and to create social change in society through advocacy, education and training.
  • Current level: 1
  • Year 2013-14
  • Sector -Women Empowerment
  • Area: Hyderabad
  • www.sanakalpforwomen.org

The issue:Indian Society is primarily male dominated leading to an inferior status for women. This is the primary reason for a high number of incidences of sexual violence against women. Given the customs, traditions, & lack of gender equality, the survivor does not speak out fearing social stigma. This further encourages violence and increased incidence of rape. This has led to the need to create a society where women are honoured, valued and free from sexual abuse.

The idea: Sankalp offers services free of charge to women seeking to heal from sexual violence. The services include Sexual Abuse Crisis Centre, Medical Aid/ Support, Support & Guidance with Law Enforcement Agencies, Legal Aid/ Advocacy, Counselling and Therapy, Education & Awareness Training Programmes, Networking and Partnership Programmes.

How it started: The December 16th 2012 gangrape of Nirbhaya had a deep impact on Anita and Sarah. This led to organize a candlelight vigil at People’s Plaza, Necklace Road, Hyderabad to gather like-minded individuals who want to make a change in the status of women. The passion and enthusiasm shown along with the need to sustain the momentum had motivated them to start the Trust and open a crisis centre.

The team: Anita is currently working as a communications consultant. She is a certified trainer in Child Sexual Abuse from Arpan and has done a course on social entrepreneurship from CSIM. Sarah Mathews has been working in the field of Education, Training and Content Writing for over 18 years. Sarah’s interest has been in 2 fields –Cultural Heritage of India and Empowerment and Uplift of Women.

Sankalp and UnLtd Hyderabad Anita and Sarah asked UnLtd Hyderabad to support them with:

  • 1. Mentoring for better programme impact.
  • 2. Training in fund raising & proposals.
  • 3. Networking with like-minded organizations & individuals.
