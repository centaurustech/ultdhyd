  • Sector - Livelihood
  • Area: Hyderabad

To empower Terracotta artisan groups

The issue: Terracotta artisans across the country face problems such as seasonal livelihood, low wages for their products, Consignment/Credit model, zero product diversification and design, lack of exposure to technology, and no marketing.

The idea: Color D Earth identifies and collaborates with terracotta artisans who are spread out in towns, rural and urban India. CDE empowers them by giving them the true value of their products and using fair trade practices, by helping them finish the product to a refined level where it is easily marketable to the urban market and by ensuring a sustainable livelihood for them.

The team: Hema Subramanyam, a student of Psychology started her career in career counselling and management consulting 23 years ago. She started working with artisans on terracotta jewellery in 2003.

Color D earth and UnLtd Hyderabad: Hema asked UnLtd Hyderabad to mentor her on how to measure impact and also on capacity building.
