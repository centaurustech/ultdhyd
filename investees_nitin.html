  • Nithin Prem and Phaneendra
  • Operation Innovation: To develop students as problem solvers and market creators.
  • Current level: 1
  • Year 2013-14
  • Sector - Youth
  • Area: Hyderabad

The issue: Students in many engineering colleges don't have proper resources and platforms to initiate, develop and express their ideas.

The idea: Operation Innovation aims to create an ecosystem which provides a platform for innovation and entrepreneurship for students, besides encouraging them to tackle the practical problems and converting them into problem solvers & market creators.

How it started: When Nithin had met the National Innovation Foundation executives,he learnt that there are 1,40,000 innovations from school dropouts and rural farmers,which made him wonder why there are not even a 100 innovations from the 1.5 million engineering students graduating every year? This motivated him to address the critical gap between what is taught and how the subject is effectively applied in day to day life through OI.

The team: Nithin Prem and his team are final year engineering students from Ananthapur.

OI and UnLtd Hyderabad:Nithin Prem and Phanendra approached UnLtd Hyderabad while their project was in ideation stage to help them with the subject of social entrepreneurship and the start-up finance. They needed support in coaching and building a network.
