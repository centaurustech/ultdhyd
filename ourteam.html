Our Team

UnLtd Hyderabad team comprises of dedicated and experienced professionals, entrepreneurs and social entrepreneurs. The team has years of experience in Our Programme and ecosystem development for early-stage social entrepreneurs.

Raj Janagam Founder

Raj Janagam is leading the social entrepreneurship movement in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana through the largest network of social entrepreneurs in the region. Prior to UnLtd Hyderabad he co-founded and led Cycle Chalao!, the pioneering bicycle sharing system of India. Raj has also worked with several notable non-profit, for-profit, and government institutions at local and national levels.

Featured in the Forbes, The Guardian, Fast Company and Outlook Business Magazines, Raj has been an acclaimed social entrepreneur, a fellow at Unreasonable Institute and a former UnLtd India investee. Raj has a clear vision to accelerate the impact of change-makers in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, and UnLtd Hyderabad is his vehicle to achieve that. UnLtd Hyderabad is on a mission to find, fund and support over 100 early stage social entrepreneurs by 2018.

Swapnil Barai, Program Manager

Swapnil has been engaged with non-profits and social ventures, and have strong expertise in rural development, program management and operations. Apart from full time stints, he has been consultant to various non-profits, businesses and academic institutions in the areas of program planning & development, trainings and capacity building. At UnLtd Hyderabad, he leads and manages Knowledge Management & Research, Scouting functions of the organization and assists in coaching of social entrepreneurs and their financial reviews.

Shivani Madiraju Program Coordinator

Shivani has been associated with a non-profit organization in Delhi for 18 months wherein she worked on Resource mobilization and Networking. A graduate in Biotechnology she has keen interest in learning and catalyzing the effectiveness of social entrepreneurs in the region. She has a strong management perspective on setting up efficient management systems leading to building a robust organization. At UnLtd Hyderabad, Shivani leads and coordinates Our Programme program for the investees.

Shravan Chandrasekaran Operations Catalyst

An engineer by qualification, Shravan is establishing processes and systems to scale operations at UnLtd Hyderabad. He has worked for leading high-technology companies such as Honeywell and Xilinx, where he designed and developed product defect tracking automation frameworks. He has a keen interest in measuring that which is difficult to quantify. Shravan is passionate about creating and measuring social impact, and has worn many hats before. He has been an English teacher, an event organizer and a volunteer. Shravan is a StartingBloc Fellow and has also been selected for the prestigious SBI Youth for India fellowship program.

At UnLtd Hyderabad, he is leading efforts on Strategic Partnerships and Marketing. He is also leading the efforts to set up management systems and processes to scale the organization.

Sai Suryanath Singh Accounts & Legal Police

Surya, a Commerce and an Arts graduate, has expertise in accounting, auditing and taxation. He has over three years of experience in leading teams for auditing public-listed companies, and companies framed under SEZ & EOU schemes from different regions of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. At UnLtd Hyderabad, he is leading financial management.
