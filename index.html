  • UnLtd Hyderabad

    UnLtd Hyderabad fills a critical gap in the early-stage support for social entrepreneurs in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. UnLtd Hyderabad provides seed-funding and intensive hands-on support to social entrepreneurs to launch their ventures.

  • Our Impact

    Incubated 16 entrepreneurs
    Impacted more than 42,000 lives
    Created 45 jobs
    Our alumni investees raised Rs. 6,41,702 last year.
    Our goal is to accelerate impact of over 100 social entrepreneurs across Andhra Pradesh & Telangana in the next 5 years.

  • What we do

    We work with early-stage social entrepreneurs to help them:
    Develop as leaders
    Accelerate their impact
    Prepare their organizations for scaling and further investment
