Our Offering

  1. You are here:
  2. Home
  3. Our Programme
  4. Our Offering

Our program offers a yearlong package of:

  • Seed funding: to pay for critical early start-up costs and support for your venture as you build it to a sustainable level
  • Hands-on coaching and training: on business and financial planning, fundraising, legal structures, governance, marketing and HR management
  • High-value connections: linking you with mentors, peers and experts in their field, and further funders and investors
Apply Now

We have three levels of support.

Level 1 support is designed for social entrepreneurs to start or pilot a new initiative with a seed funding upto, 80,000 Rs. along with an average of 160 hours of hands-on support over the course of a year.

Level 2 support is designed to propel existing high-potential early-stage project with a seed funding upto Rs. 2 Lakh along with an average of 220 hours of hands-on support over one year.

Growth Challenge (previously called Level 3) is designed to provide social business with the loan capital it needs to grow. Up to Rs. 20 Lakhs of loan financing is available, repayable over three years. Also up to Rs. 12 Lakhs of soft loan is available for talent for non-profits, part of which is repayable over three years. This level includes customized support package over the period of the loan. This is an initiative of UnLtd India and is accessible via UnLtd Hyderabad's nomination.

Our hands on support includes personal coaching and group trainings on business and financial planning, fundraising, legal structures, governance, marketing and HR management.
